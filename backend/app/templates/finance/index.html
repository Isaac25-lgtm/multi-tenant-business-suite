How This Page Works
  • Finance Overview: Monitor loan portfolio at a glance - click any card to view details (display only)
  • Quick Actions: Start all data entry here - add clients, issue loans, record payments
Loan Processing Flow
  • For Individual Loans: First add the client in Clients, then create loan in Individual Loans
  • For Group Loans: Go directly to Group Loans - enter group name, members, and loan details
  • Agreement Preview: Review all details before issuing - you can add member details and upload collateral
  • Recording Payments: Click on any loan to view details and record payments
  • Track Progress: Use Payments to see all recent payment history
Access Permissions
  • Employees: Can only add/edit entries for today and yesterday
  • Managers: Can view and edit entries for the past 30 days, with date filtering
